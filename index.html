The details that earn their place

01

USB charging port

A dual charging interface built into the shell keeps your phone and devices powered while you move through the terminal.

02

Hidden phone holder

A tucked-away stand folds out to hold your phone steady — hands-free for boarding passes, calls or a show at the gate.

03

Embedded cup holder

A built-in holder keeps your coffee or water steady and spill-free, so your drink rides on top instead of in your hand.

04

Quick-entry front pocket

A front laptop compartment opens on its own, so you reach your essentials without unpacking the whole case at security.

05

Silent 360° wheels

Anti-skid spinner wheels roll flat in every direction and stay quiet across marble, tarmac and cobblestone alike.

06

Three-layer composite shell

A composite PC shell stacks wear-resistant, buffering and tough layers — impact-resistant, so it flexes and springs back.
